Who We Are

Green Globe Guardian is a Non-Governmental Organization headquartered in Nakuru, Kenya. We focus on mitigating climate change through impactful programs that engage communities and stakeholders globally.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable solutions towards the mitigation of climate change through community-driven and innovative approaches.

Our Vision

To partner with communities and stakeholders in implementing strategic climate change mitigation programs for long-term global sustainability.

Core Values

Integrity Restoration Preservation Protection Innovation Social Responsibility
Making a Difference

Our Impact

Combating Climate Change

Through our programs, we aim at combating climate change by sensitizing members of the public on the need to conserve the environment.

Our Approach

We strive to meet our objectives through direct and indirect approaches. We work directly with communities through initiatives like tree planting, while also sensitizing communities on climate change and practical solutions.

Sensitization

Raising awareness and educating communities through campaigns, school programs, and outreach initiatives.

Sustainable Farming

Supporting farmers with organic practices, crop diversification, and water conservation techniques.

Renewable Energy

Promoting solar, wind, and hydropower adoption while supporting community energy projects.

Waste Management

Encouraging Reduce, Reuse, Recycle practices and promoting eco-friendly waste solutions.
